How does express entry work

Express Entry is an online system introduced by the Canadian government
in 2015 which is used to manage Canadian permanent residency
applications under three federal economic immigration
programs.

  • Federal Skilled Worker

This program is for skilled workers with foreign work experience
who want to immigrate to Canada permanently.

  • Federal Skilled Trades

The Federal Skilled Trades Program is for skilled workers who want
to become permanent residents based on being qualified in a
skilled trade.

  • Canadian Experience Class

The Canadian Experience Class is for skilled workers who have
Canadian work experience and want to become permanent residents.
